Guess Who?

The 2-player mystery face game

How to Play

  1. Both players open this page on their own device.
  2. Each secretly picks one character — don't show the other player!
  3. Take turns asking yes / no questions out loud.
  4. Eliminate characters that can't be the answer.
  5. When you're ready — hit Guess! to win.
